Fosense · Company & product profile

Fosense builds clinician-controlled clinical evidence technology for child and adolescent mental health and neurodevelopmental care.

Source-linked information organization, contradiction mapping, and draft clinical materials — without autonomous diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or clinical decision-making.

Fosense develops Fosight, a proprietary clinician-controlled platform for organizing multi-source clinical information in child and adolescent mental health and neurodevelopmental services. Fosight helps clinicians structure fragmented information from parents, caregivers, teachers, questionnaires, prior reports, clinical observations, parent portal inputs, and optional structured gameplay interactions. The product preserves source traceability, surfaces gaps and discrepancies, and prepares information for clinician review without autonomous interpretation or decision-making.

Fosight's Adventure is an optional structured gameplay intake module within Fosight — not the full product and not a standalone diagnostic tool. Fosight is modular by design: clinics use the core evidence workspace and add modules for workflow assistance, parent intake, adaptive questionnaires, optional structured gameplay, clinic administration, and case handoffs.

Clinician review

Fosight prepares structured draft materials and contradiction maps for clinician review, editing, and confirmation. It does not determine clinical significance or make autonomous clinical decisions.

Why it matters

Child and adolescent behavioral cases rarely arrive as one clean story. Parents, teachers, questionnaires, prior reports, and clinical observations may describe different versions of the same child.

Fosight preserves these differences instead of flattening them into a single summary, helping clinicians see what was reported, where it came from, and how sources diverge.

Fosight is designed to operate as a standalone clinical workspace or as an EHR-adjacent evidence layer. FHIR-compatible interoperability, security, privacy, regulatory-readiness, and validation pathways are active workstreams.

System boundaries

Fosight does not diagnose or generate diagnostic classifications.
Fosight does not generate risk scores, clinical recommendations, or treatment recommendations.
Fosight does not replace clinical judgment or make autonomous treatment decisions.
Fosight organizes and highlights source-provided information; clinical significance remains for clinician review.
All interpretation, diagnosis, and treatment decisions remain with the clinician.

Status

Fosense is advancing Fosight toward controlled pilot readiness. Fosight includes an early Clinical Workflow Assistant, a working Parent Portal, iPhone parent access, role-based clinic workflows, and case handoff workflows. Optional modules for structured gameplay intake, adaptive questionnaires, and EHR-adjacent deployment are in active development. Security, privacy, regulatory-readiness, validation pathways, and FHIR-compatible interoperability are active workstreams.
